Fast Fashion

Fast fashion is a huge contributer to air pollution, landfill waste, and can severly deplete our non-renewable resources.
We can choose to be more mindful of where we shop and who we support.

Mindful Shopping

Upcycling your old clothing is a great way of recycling clothes you may have wanted to get rid of.
Donating, shopping at local businesses, and buying secondhand clothing are all wonderful ways of shopping sustainably.
